COUNTER STRIIKE

  • Counter-Strike is an objective-based, multiplayer tactical first-person shooter. Two opposing teams—the Terrorists and the Counter Terrorists—compete in game modes to complete objectives, such as securing a location to plant or defuse a bomb and rescuing or guarding hostages.

CS2/CSGO

  • differences between CS2 and CS:GO include: Game Engine: CS2 uses the Source 2 game engine, offering better visuals and effects. CS:GO used the original Source engine for over 11 years.

CS2 Maps

  • Counter Strike maps offer a diverse game landscape, shaping player strategies with their unique designs, environments, and texture changes. The changes in Counter Strike game maps reflect both technological advancements in textures and the evolving preferences of the gaming community

CSGO Maps

  • Maps are the stages on which Counter-Strike matches are played. They are all incredibly different and as such see a ton of varying styles of gameplay and strategy. Some maps even favor the Terrorists or Counter-Terrorists due to their layout.
